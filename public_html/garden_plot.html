Note

We believe that everyone should have access to green space and fresh food. If the fee for a garden plot would prevent you from participating, please contact us. We have a limited number of sliding-scale and no-cost plots available each season.

Information About Garden Plots

  • Plots are assigned on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • Priority is given to residents who live within a one-mile radius of the garden.
  • Each gardener is responsible for maintaining their own plot and participating in at least two community workdays per season.
  • The annual fee for a plot is currently $25, but a small number of fee waivers are available for those who need them.
